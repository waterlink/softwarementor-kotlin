Can login after signing up as a mentor and confirming their account:

|script                                                                                                                         |
|when signing up as mentor with name|John                              |and email   |john.smith@example.org|and password|welcome|
|check                              |response                          |SUCCESS                                                 |
|check                              |last confirmation email is sent to|john.smith@example.org                                  |
|$confirmation_code=                |last confirmation email's code                                                             |
|when confirming email with code    |$confirmation_code                                                                         |
|check                              |response                          |SUCCESS                                                 |
|when logging in with name          |John                              |and password|welcome                                    |
|check                              |response                          |SUCCESS                                                 |
|check                              |current user                      |John                                                    |
|check                              |current mentor                    |John                                                    |
|check                              |current mentee                    |NOBODY                                                  |

Can not login without confirmation of their account:

|script                                                                                                |
|when signing up as mentor with name|Sarah         |and email   |sarah@example.org|and password|welcome|
|check                              |response      |SUCCESS                                            |
|when logging in with name          |Sarah         |and password|welcome                               |
|check                              |response      |EMAIL_NOT_CONFIRMED                                |
|check                              |current user  |NOBODY                                             |
|check                              |current mentor|NOBODY                                             |
|check                              |current mentee|NOBODY                                             |
